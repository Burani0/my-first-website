Lenovo partners with Computers Planet as an Authorized reseller in Uganda

Lenovo, the global technology giant, has partnered with Computers Planet to become an authorized reseller of Lenovo products in Uganda. This partnership marks a significant milestone for both companies, as Lenovo increases its footprint in the Ugandan market while Computers Planet expands its product offerings.

Computers Planet is a leading provider of IT solutions in Uganda, specializing in the supply of hardware, software, and networking solutions. With a strong reputation for delivering quality products and exceptional customer service, Computers Planet has established itself as a trusted name in the Ugandan IT industry.

As an authorized reseller of Lenovo products, Computers Planet will have access to Lenovo’s extensive range of laptops, desktops, tablets, and accessories. This will enable the company to offer its customers a wider range of options to meet their diverse needs while ensuring that they receive authentic Lenovo products backed by Lenovo’s warranty and technical support.

The partnership between Lenovo and Computers Planet is expected to enhance the availability and accessibility of Lenovo products in Uganda. This is particularly significant given the increased demand for reliable and affordable computing solutions in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic, which has accelerated the adoption of digital technologies across various sectors.

Speaking about the partnership, Lenovo East Africa’s General Manager, Eric Wambugu, said, “We are excited to partner with Computers Planet to expand our reach in Uganda. This partnership is part of our commitment to providing customers with quality, reliable, and affordable computing solutions that meet their evolving needs.”

For Computers Planet , the partnership with Lenovo is a testament to the company’s dedication to delivering innovative and high-quality IT solutions to its customers. “We are proud to partner with Lenovo, a global leader in technology, to bring their products to our customers in Uganda. This partnership aligns with our mission to provide our customers with the best possible IT solutions for their business needs,” said Computers Planet’s Managing Director, Wasswa Charles

In conclusion, the partnership between Lenovo and Computers Planet is a win-win for both companies and their customers. Lenovo will benefit from Computers Planet ’s extensive distribution network and exceptional customer service, while Computers Planet will be able to offer its customers a wider range of quality computing solutions backed by Lenovo’s expertise and support
